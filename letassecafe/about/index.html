About Us

Le Tasse Café opened in August 2010 in historic Cornerstone district.

Café owners Jonathan, Meryl, Pat & Eva are long-time proud residents of the neighborhood.
So far, they happen to be faceless in order to stand-in
for any photographic list of incredible team members,
but it turns out that they don't mind one bit.

Jonathan is our office clerk.
He often travels to France where he visits family, and he has been known to compete in tour de France.

Meryl is our resident baker.
When she has time off, she
enjoys camping and playing dodgeball.

Patrick is head chef at
Le Tasse Café In addition
to creating delicious meals, he enjoys participating in Chess tournaments and he's
an avid book collector.

Eva is our in-house designer.
She owns SpaceCultDesign just down the street and she manages events at the Café, including art show openings and entertainment.
